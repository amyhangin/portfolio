Introduction

Amy Hang in is a travel blog based in the United States of America. The blog is not inclusive of only domestic travel within the U.S. The website features travel experiences by Amy Hang in various countries in this world that we are living in.

Here is what you can expect to learn on amyhangin.com:

  • Happy go-lucky stories
  • Unfortunate moments in a foreign country
  • Local activities
  • Food recommendations
  • The best travel items to have
  • Tips and tricks on traveling on a budget
About the Traveler

The content shared on Amy Hang in is produced and written by Amy Hang. Her professional career started after graduating with a Bachelor's in Digital Media and Music Business. With a love of travel and music, she decided to move to South Korea and teach English abroad. During her 4.5 years living in South Korea, she experienced many life experiences dealing with a language barrier different cultures of people, and living on her own. Being in the country with a fast-growing entertainment industry, the experiences of going to music concerts and festivals were abundant and exciting. There was so much to see and learn. Food is another topic that is loved. Whenever and wherever she travels, food is a connection to the culture and the destination she visits.

As of mid-2022, her focus is to develop software that could help communities in need. In order to improve her skills, she is studying programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, and many more. She is also striving to create content to share with others about travel, food, and culture.

Countries Traveled

The first time she fell in love with travel was on a family vacation to Canada at the age of 12. On a ferry staring out into the water, that was the first time she felt a sense of peace. "I want to see the world," the thought that popped into her mind at that moment. The feeling of the ocean breeze, the whispers of the ocean water, and the soft blurs of the blue sky were all engrained into her visual memory until this day.

Here are all the countries she traveled to without family. Either met up with a friend or solo:

  • Ireland
  • South Korea
  • Hong Kong
  • Japan
  • Thailand
  • Singapore
  • Jeju Island
